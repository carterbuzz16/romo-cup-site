Tournament Info

How the bracket works.

  • Format: Double elimination means a team has to lose twice before it is out.
  • Progression: Winners stay on the top path while one-loss teams fight through the losers bracket.
  • Championship Saturday: The final stretch runs through Games 5-9, with Game 9 only needed if the losers bracket winner takes Game 8.
