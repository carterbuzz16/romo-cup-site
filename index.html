Romo's Story

William “Romo” Romanowski left behind a note that continues to guide his family and community with one simple challenge: keep encouraging others and keep moving forward.

In his honor, his family created inspirational bracelets to share comfort with people walking through hard seasons. The Romo Cup was built from that same mission, turning remembrance into support, community, and lasting impact. Today, that mission helps fund the Auburn University endowed scholarship, the Will "Romo" Scholarship, awarded annually to a fraternity man.

Every donation helps carry Romo's legacy forward through the Will "Romo" Scholarship at Auburn University.
